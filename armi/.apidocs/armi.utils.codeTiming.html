armi.utils.codeTiming.timed(*args)[source]¶

Decorate functions to measure how long they take.

Examples

@timed # your timer will be called the module+method name
def mymethod(stuff):
    do stuff

@timed('call my timer this instead')
def mymethod2(stuff)
   do even more stuff
